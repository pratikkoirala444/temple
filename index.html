About Temple

A must visit place for religious people during their birthday. The place houses the 8 immortals of the hindu mythology/scriptures and is revered by Hindus from different cultures, caste and race. It is a relatively newer temple as compared to others but has been growing with humble donations.

It's location is Near Namuna Vidya Mandir, Madhumara Path, Biratnagar 56700.
